Flesh Talk

(Idea/ Paper Presentation)

Rules:-

  • Each flash talk is for 3 minutes, highlighting the key results presented in the poster.
  • The number of slides should be restricted to 3 content slides (excluding the title slide and acknowledgment slide).
  • The aspect ratio of the slides should be maintained at 16:9.
  • The presentations should be uploaded in ppt/pptx format only to the online repository at (…………………..) on or before April, 2024 without fail.
  • The poster should be prepared using the same guidelines as given under "Information for Poster Presentations".
  • Presenting the poster is mandatory for all the flash talks.
  • Communication: Did the presentation communicate the research effectively?
  • Importance: Did the presentation effectively communicate the innovation and/or significance of the research?
  • Engagement: Did the presentation make the audience want to know more?
