Gaming

Rules:-

  • The tournament organizer creates a custom room within the free fire game client. This room will have specific settings chosen by the organizer
  • Players who wish to participate in the tournament must be invited to the custom room by the organizer or have the room ID and password provided to them
  • Teams are typically composed of four players, although the organizer may allow for variations in team size
  • Rules against cheating, hacking, exploiting bugs, or any other unfair advantages apply. Players found in violation of these rules may be disqualified from the tournament
  • Rules regarding how matches start, what happens in case of disconnections, and how to handle technical issues during matches should be established
  • Players should adhere to a code of conduct set by the tournament organizer, which may include rules about language, behavior, and interaction with other players.
  • The organizer is responsible for managing the custom room, including starting matches, enforcing rules, and resolving any issues that arise during gameplay.
  • Participants are prohibited from using any third-party software, hacks, scripts, or other tools that provide an unfair advantage.
  • Team killing is not allowed; work together with your teammates
